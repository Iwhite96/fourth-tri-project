Introducing

Fourth Trimester Services, your trusted partner in postpartum care in Northeast Ohio. We specialize in providing comprehensive support and guidance to mothers and families during the crucial postpartum period. With our experienced team of birth/postpartum workers, we offer personalized care, ensuring a smooth transition into parenthood. At Fourth Trimester Services, we understand the unique needs of new mothers and their babies. Our compassionate approach focuses on nurturing both physical and emotional well-being, empowering families to thrive during this transformative time. Whether it's breastfeeding support, newborn care education, or postpartum recovery assistance, our dedicated team is committed to delivering exceptional care and guidance tailored to your individual needs. Experience a supportive and holistic approach to postpartum care with Fourth Trimester Services.

About

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a global movement that redefines the postpartum journey. At the heart of our vision lies the transformation of postpartum into a time of rest and healing. We imagine a future where every woman and family is enveloped in a nurturing embrace, where birthing signifies not just the arrival of a new life but a rebirth of the self. We see a world where our sacred spaces become beacons of hope, driving forward a legacy of healthy, educated, and united families.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the postpartum experience, embracing birthing as a process of rebirth and renewal by creating a safe haven of expert care, accessible education, and unwavering support, we envision a world where families emerge stronger. For every woman, man, and family, regardless of background, we are devoted to ushering in a new era of empowered parenthood, where freedom, health, and holistic well-being reign.
